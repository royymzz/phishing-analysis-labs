## Scenario
A phishing email impersonating a payment service was analyzed.

## Red Flags Identified
- Urgent language
- Generic greeting
- Suspicious sender domain
- Fake login link

## Analysis Steps
- Domain checked using whois
- DNS records reviewed
- Link inspected without visiting

## Outcome
The email was confirmed as a credential harvesting attempt.

## Mitigation
- Block malicious domains
- User awareness training
- Email filtering rules
